Againly (“the app”) is a personal task and reminder application designed to help users manage recurring reminders locally on their device.

Data Collection

Againly does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers.

All tasks, reminders, and app settings are stored locally on the user’s device and never leave the device.

Data Usage

The app uses the following device features:

Notifications (to remind users about tasks)

Local storage (to save tasks and preferences on the device)

This data is used only to provide core app functionality.

Third-Party Services

Againly does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

Google Play Billing is used solely to process in-app purchases and subscriptions. Google may collect data as described in their own privacy policy.

Data Sharing

Againly does not share user data with any third parties.

Children’s Privacy

Againly is not designed for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

User Control

Users can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app.
